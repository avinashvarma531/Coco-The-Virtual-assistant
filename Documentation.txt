This is Coco, a custom Windows 10 assistant for doing simple tasks such as,

1.Tell a joke.
2.Shutdown or hibernate or lock or restart PC.
3.Give an advice. 
4.Give meaning of a word.
5.Send an email.
6.Give a quote.
7.Open windows settings.
8.Open web sites
9.Switch on or off toggles(eg: Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, night light etc).

NOTE:
	All the required modules should be downloaded for assistant to work correctly.
